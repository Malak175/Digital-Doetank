Intro Digital Doetank

Why We Exist

We live in a new era. A time when digital choices are not only technical or financial, but also geopolitical, strategic, and societal. The past few years have made it clear: digital autonomy is not a luxury, it is a necessity.

But… where do you start? What is realistic? What fits your organization? That’s exactly where Digital Doetank comes in. We help you navigate, make choices, and take action. So your organization is not only prepared for the future, but also helps build it.

How We Work

Structure of the Organization

Mission at the Center

Digital Doetank is organized according to the principle of steward ownership: the mission is central. We focus on societal impact, not shareholder value.

Open Source & Public Knowledge

What we develop, we share. What is not confidential is made available to others. This strengthens not only organizations, but also the broader ecosystem.

Independent but Connected

We are fully independent from suppliers. Yet we work closely with a growing network of European technology providers. This allows us to advise objectively and help find alternatives that suit you.

Co-Creation as Standard

We believe real impact only happens when you collaborate. That’s why we always work with people from your organization in the project team. Knowledge sticks, ownership grows, and together we make the difference.
